Curriculum

  • Week 1
    Fundamentals
      - Overview of Machine Learning concepts - Introduction to Python programming - Setting up the development environment - Hands-on exercises with basic ML algorithms
  • Week 2
    Supervised Learning Algorithms
    - Understanding linear regression - Exploring logistic regression - Implementing decision trees and random forests - Building and evaluating support vector machines (SVM) - Applying k-nearest neighbors (KNN)
  • Week 3
    Unsupervised Learning Algorithms
    - Introduction to clustering algorithms - Implementing k-means clustering - Exploring hierarchical clustering - Understanding dimensionality reduction techniques (PCA) - Hands-on exercises with unsupervised learning
  • Week 4
    Deep Learning and Neural Networks
    - Introduction to deep learning - Building neural networks using TensorFlow/Keras - Understanding convolutional neural networks (CNN) - Exploring recurrent neural networks (RNN) - Hands-on projects with deep learning models
  • Week 5
    Natural Language Processing (NLP)
    - Introduction to NLP and text preprocessing - Implementing sentiment analysis - Building text classification models - Exploring language generation with recurrent neural networks - Hands-on exercises with NLP applications
  • Week 6
    Reinforcement Learning
    - Understanding reinforcement learning concepts - Implementing Q-learning algorithms - Exploring deep reinforcement learning - Building an agent to play games - Hands-on projects with reinforcement learning
  • Week 7
    Earning Money through Freelancing
    - Introduction to freelancing platforms - Building a strong freelance profile - Identifying ML projects in demand - Pricing and negotiation strategies - Showcasing your skills through a portfolio
  • Week 8
    Hands-on Experience in ML
    - Collaborative projects with fellow learners - Solving real-world ML problems - Implementing end-to-end ML pipelines - Working with large datasets - Gaining practical ML experience
  • Week 9
    Project Expo
    - Preparing and presenting your ML projects - Showcasing your skills to potential employers - Networking with industry professionals - Receiving feedback and suggestions for improvement
  • Week 10
    Internship Opportunities and Opensource
    - Exploring internship programs in ML - Applying for ML internships - Gaining industry experience and mentorship - Adding valuable credentials to your resume - Contribute to Opensource Projects
